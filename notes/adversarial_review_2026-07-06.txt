================ (B) PHASE-SHIFT EXTRACTION (scripts/phase_shifts.py) ================

B1. Quantization condition — VERIFIED CORRECT. p*Nx + 2*delta(p) = 2*pi*n with Nx = ns//2 spatial sites is the standard 1+1d CM-frame condition for two IDENTICAL BOSONS (even channel only; equivalent to e^{ipL} e^{2i delta} = 1, or "2delta(k) = -kL mod 2pi" as used in massive-Schwinger meson-meson scattering, hep-lat/9709158, hep-lat/9904015, 2112.15228, and Luscher-Wolff). Derivation check: symmetric relative wavefunction cos(p x + delta) with psi'(L/2)=0 gives pL + 2delta = 2pi m, all integer m — no extra symmetry factor, no odd channel. The two mesons are the same band-1 particle (fermion-pair bound state) → identical bosons; units (k per spatial site, L = nx) consistent. Sign convention: level below free ⇒ delta > 0 ⇒ attractive — consistent. Arithmetic of all three headline points re-verified to 4 decimals from the saved npz.

Deep 60-state spectra (data/deep_levels_ns{8,10}.npz) show 5 (ns=8) / 6 (ns=10) in-window P=0 levels against only 3 free MM levels per volume.
